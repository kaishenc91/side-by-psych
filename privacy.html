Website privacy

A small amount of data, handled with care.

This notice explains what happens when you visit sidebypsych.com or use its contact links. It applies to the public website, not to clinical records or the separate confidentiality arrangements for psychotherapy.

Last updated: 24 July 2026

What this website collects

The website itself does not create an account for you and does not store the information typed into the enquiry form on a website server. When you choose “Send via WhatsApp” or “Send via Email”, the website prepares a message and opens the relevant app on your device. The message is only sent when you choose to send it there.

Please avoid including highly sensitive health information in an initial website, WhatsApp or email enquiry. These channels are not intended for emergencies.

Optional website analytics

With your permission, the website uses Google Analytics to understand basic use of the site, such as which page was viewed, broad device information, general geographic area and whether a contact or language link was selected. This helps us improve information that visitors find useful.

Analytics is off until you choose “Allow analytics”. We do not intentionally send your name, telephone number, email address, enquiry message, selected support area or other clinical information to analytics. Your choice is stored on your device and can be changed by clearing this site's browser storage or using the privacy settings shown on the website.

Contacting the centre

If you contact Side by Psych through WhatsApp, email, telephone or another external service, that service's privacy terms also apply. Information needed to respond to an enquiry or arrange an appointment will be handled for that purpose.

External links

The website links to services such as WhatsApp, email, Instagram and Google Maps. Side by Psych is not responsible for the privacy practices of those separate platforms.

Your questions

For questions about this website notice, email hello@sidebypsych.com. For privacy questions relating to psychotherapy or clinical records, please raise them directly with the clinical psychologist.

网站会收集什么？

本网站不会为您建立帐号，也不会把您在询问表格填写的内容储存在网站伺服器。当您选择“通过WhatsApp发送”或“通过电邮发送”时，网站只会整理好讯息，并在您的装置打开相关应用程式；是否发送，仍由您决定。

第一次联系时，请尽量不要通过网站、WhatsApp或电邮发送高度敏感的健康资料。这些渠道也不适用于紧急求助。

自愿开启的网站分析

在获得您的同意后，网站会使用Google Analytics了解基本使用情况，例如浏览了哪一页、大致的装置类别和地区，以及是否点击联系或语言链接。这些资料帮助我们改善访客真正需要的内容。

除非您选择“允许网站分析”，否则分析功能不会启动。我们不会刻意把您的姓名、电话号码、电邮地址、询问信息、所选择的困扰范围或其他临床资料传送给分析工具。您的选择会保存在当前装置；清除本网站的浏览器储存资料，或使用网站上的隐私设定，即可重新选择。

联系伴旅

如果您通过WhatsApp、电邮、电话或其他外部服务联系我们，也会同时受到该服务本身的隐私条款约束。为了回复询问或安排预约而取得的资料，只会按相关用途处理。

外部链接

本网站会连接至WhatsApp、电邮、Instagram与Google Maps等服务。这些平台有各自的隐私做法，伴旅无法代为负责。

如果您有疑问

关于本网站的隐私说明，可电邮至 hello@sidebypsych.com。若问题涉及心理治疗或临床记录，请直接向临床心理师提出。
