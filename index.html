Our approach

Professional care that feels grounded and human.

Side by Psych is built around the idea that healing is not something people have to do alone. Sessions are designed to be collaborative, respectful and practical, with support shaped around the person in front of us.

  • Confidential, appointment-based support for a focused and private experience.
  • Accessible mental health services for individuals, families, schools and workplaces.
  • Clear communication through WhatsApp, phone or email before booking.
  • Flexible in-person and online options to suit different needs and schedules.
